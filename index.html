About

The students at IIT Dharwad are encouraged to be active participants in sports year-round. The institute has numerous sporting events including Inter Hostels General Championships, Inter IIT competitions, etc. The support provided by IIT Dharwad towards Athletic Excellence includes offering athletes high-quality sports facilities (eg: basketball courts, volleyball courts, soccer & cricket fields) which are continually being improved on and in development swimming pool. A second means of promoting teamwork, discipline and Good Sportsmanship within IIT Dharwad’s community is through the opportunity for Faculty to partner with students in developing their Skills and creating an enlivening format for Athletics at the Institute.
